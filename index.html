💡 Tips for Learning Japanese

  • Practice writing Hiragana and Katakana daily to memorize shapes.
  • Use flashcards or apps to match kana with sounds.
  • Listen to native pronunciation to improve your accent.
  • Learn simple words and read children’s books in Japanese.
  • Practice speaking out loud every day, even a few minutes.
  • Try associating kana with images or mnemonic tricks.
